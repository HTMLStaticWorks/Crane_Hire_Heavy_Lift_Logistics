Overview

Unmatched Stability for Heavy Terrain

Our fleet of crawler cranes offers superior lifting capabilities on unprepared job sites. With load capacities ranging from 100T to 3000T, these machines are the backbone of wind energy installations, refinery turnarounds, and mega-infrastructure projects.

Crawler cranes provide excellent pick-and-carry capabilities, allowing operators to move with suspended loads safely across the site, drastically reducing the need for multiple crane setups.

Technical Specifications

Available in classes from 100 Tons to 3,000 Tons. Our heavy lift configurations include superlift attachments, luffing jibs, and derrick systems to maximize load charts for long-radius picks.

Main boom lengths up to 240 meters (787 feet). Compatible with fixed jibs, luffing jibs, and wind power specific boom systems to clear wide turbine nacelles.

Extra-wide track pads and meticulously calculated ground bearing pressure (GBP) data provided with every lift plan, ensuring safe operation on varying soil conditions. Matting services available.
